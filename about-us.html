ABOUT THE JOKE LOVERS
Besides pursuing my B.Tech at the renowned institute having status of NATIONAL IMPORTANCE in INDIA, I generally explore many places and meet with many persons. I observe a common thing among all of them is that many of them, approx. 80%, are feeling under depression with the stress of daily life. So our team has decided to contribute a small effort towards the simple aim of 'Smile Spreading'. With this motto, we have endeavoured to develop such a platform that can fulfill it and consequently 'JOKE LOVERS' has been created.
